Office Hours

To see our dragons in person, you can visit our store between 9AM through 5PM, Monday through Friday. We are unavailable on most federal holidays. Weekends can be booked for birthday parties or other private events. Children attending such events should be advised not to wear tiaras or too much metal as the dragons may mistake them for princesses or knights.

We can hold dragons by request, but only for a maximum of one week before we will re-list them for sale.
